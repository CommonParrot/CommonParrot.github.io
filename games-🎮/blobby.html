Blobby
...

(2023)

Blobby is a puzzle platformer with unique physics about
a man who became a blob, freeing imprisoned frogs from a
alien space station, which has fallen into chaos, while searching for
a cure to his blobness!

It is my first game. I worked on it for over a year,
to learn about Game Programming, Design and Godot.
Most advice is to do a lot of small projects first...
But this is a large one, consisting of a lot of small projects
glued togetherðŸ™ƒ

For now it is only a Demo, which will go public soon.
The mechanics are complete for now, but I plan on
incorporating new art, new levels and bug fixes.
I might even make it a full game if the demo gains some interest.
